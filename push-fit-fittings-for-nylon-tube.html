  • Visual Shape: Push-in pneumatic fitting for nylon or polyurethane tube.
  • Key Checks: Confirm tube OD, thread size/type, shape and swivel requirement.
  • Common Types: Straight, elbow, tee, bulkhead, reducer, flow control and manifold.
  • Release Method: Push collar/collet in to release tube.
  • Do Not Confuse With: Compression or hydraulic fittings.

Application

  • Pneumatic Control Systems: Quickly connect nylon tube in compressed-air control circuits.
  • Automation Machinery: Used on cylinders, valves, manifolds, and robotic equipment.
  • Packaging and Food Equipment: Common in clean pneumatic systems and lightweight machinery.
  • Workshop Air Lines: Provide simple tube connections for air tools and service equipment.
  • Maintenance Repairs: Useful for fast replacement of damaged or leaking tube connections.

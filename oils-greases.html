  • Visual Check: Confirm lubricant type, viscosity/grade, thickener and compatibility.
  • Grease Checks: Look for NLGI grade, EP rating, food grade, high temperature or water resistance.
  • Oil Checks: Check ISO VG grade and whether hydraulic, gear, compressor or chain oil.
  • Application Clue: Selection depends on speed, load, temperature and contamination.
  • Do Not Confuse With: Similar-looking cartridges or bottles.

Application

  • Bearing Lubrication: Reduce friction and wear in rolling and plain bearings.
  • Chains and Conveyors: Protect chains, rollers, and guides from wear and corrosion.
  • Gearboxes: Lubricate gears, bearings, and shafts under load.
  • Food Processing: Food-grade products suit machinery used near food production areas.
  • Preventive Maintenance: Used to extend component life and reduce breakdowns in routine servicing.

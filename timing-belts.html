Identification

Identification image placeholder
Add labelled catalogue image at assets/img/identification/timing-belts.jpg
  • Visual Shape: Toothed belt with evenly spaced teeth on inside surface.
  • Key Checks: Confirm pitch, tooth profile, width, teeth/length and cord material.
  • Common Profiles: HTD, GT, AT, T, XL, L, H and metric profiles.
  • Application Clue: Used for synchronised non-slip drive.
  • Do Not Confuse With: V-belts or ribbed belts.

Application

  • Synchronous Drives: Transmit power where timing between shafts must be maintained.
  • Packaging Machinery: Provide accurate indexed movement in automated production equipment.
  • 3D Printers and CNC: Used for precise motion control and positioning.
  • Automotive: Used in engine timing systems and auxiliary synchronous drives.
  • Food and Light Industry: Clean, low-maintenance drives where chain lubrication may not be preferred.

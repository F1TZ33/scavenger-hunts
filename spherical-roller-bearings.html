Identification

  • Visual Shape: Two rows of barrel-shaped rollers with a common spherical outer raceway.
  • Self-Alignment: Tolerates shaft deflection and misalignment under heavy radial load.
  • Common Markings: 222xx, 223xx, 22xxx, 23xxx, CC, CA, E, W33, K, C3/C4.
  • Key Checks: Confirm taper/straight bore, clearance, cage and adapter sleeve requirement.
  • Do Not Confuse With: Cylindrical roller bearings.

Application

  • Mining & Construction Machinery: Used in crushers, conveyors, screens, and vibrating equipment where shock loads are common.
  • Wind Turbines: Support large shafts where high radial loads and shaft deflection occur.
  • Steel Mills: Used in continuous casting machines, trunnions, and rolling mills.
  • Pulp & Paper Processing: Suited to long rolls that can sag or deflect under load and heat.
  • Heavy Gearboxes & Fans: Applied in large gearboxes, blowers, fans, and pumps.
  • Marine & Offshore: Used in propulsion systems, winches, and offshore drilling equipment.

Identification

  • Visual Shape: Eye-shaped joint with spherical bearing and threaded shank.
  • Thread Type: Confirm male/female, LH/RH, metric/imperial and pitch.
  • Key Checks: Measure bore, thread, shank length and housing width.
  • Application Clue: Used in linkages requiring angular misalignment.
  • Do Not Confuse With: Spherical plain bearings without rod-end body.

Application

  • Linkages: Used in mechanical linkages requiring angular movement or adjustment.
  • Steering and Suspension: Common in motorsport, industrial vehicles, and steering assemblies.
  • Hydraulic Cylinders: Used at cylinder ends where alignment changes during operation.
  • Automation: Support adjustable arms, actuators, and motion-control linkages.
  • Agriculture and Machinery: Used on control rods, levers, and pivoting mechanisms.

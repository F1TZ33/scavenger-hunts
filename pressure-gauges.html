  • Visual Shape: Dial or digital gauge displaying system pressure.
  • Key Checks: Confirm range, connection, thread, dial size, liquid fill and units.
  • Mounting Style: Bottom entry, rear entry, panel mount and flange mount.
  • Application Clue: Gauge range should suit working pressure and spikes.
  • Do Not Confuse With: Temperature gauges or regulators.

Application

  • Hydraulic Systems: Monitor system pressure on pumps, power packs, and hydraulic lines.
  • Pneumatic Systems: Check compressed-air supply, regulators, and control circuits.
  • Pumps and Process Equipment: Monitor pressure in water, oil, and process systems.
  • Troubleshooting: Used to diagnose blocked filters, pressure loss, over-pressure, or system imbalance.
  • Safety and Compliance: Provide visible pressure indication for operators and maintenance staff.

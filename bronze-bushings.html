  • Visual Shape: Plain cylindrical sleeve or flanged bush made from bronze/sintered bronze.
  • Surface Style: Solid, oil-impregnated, graphite plugged, grooved or wrapped.
  • Measurement Check: Measure ID, OD, length and flange details if applicable.
  • Application Clue: Used for sliding, oscillating or low-speed rotation.
  • Do Not Confuse With: Steel-backed DU/DX or plastic bushes.

Application

  • Pivot Points: Used in hinges, linkages, and oscillating shaft locations.
  • Construction Equipment: Common in excavators, loaders, buckets, pins, and booms.
  • Agricultural Machinery: Used in implements and machinery exposed to dirt, impact, and intermittent lubrication.
  • Industrial Machinery: Suitable for low-speed, high-load rotational or sliding movement.
  • Repair and Maintenance: Often used as serviceable wear components to protect shafts and housings.
